\begin{abstract}
\noindent This document serves as the chronological laboratory report and technical foundation for Project Pienza. It documents the evolution from $N=1$ ground-truth data acquisition through rigorous data engineering, exploratory analysis, and supervised cloning, culminating in the deployment of Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) for market simulation.

The content, models, and insights presented herein are for illustrative and educational purposes and are not intended for, nor should they be used for, any commercial, financial, or operational business activities.
